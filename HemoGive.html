About Us

Welcome to our blood donation website! We are a team of passionate individuals committed to raising awareness about the importance of blood donation and making it easier for people to donate. Our goal is to connect donors with blood banks and hospitals in need of blood so that patients can receive the life-saving treatments they need.

We believe that every donation counts and can make a significant impact on someone's life. We also strive to provide educational resources to the public on the benefits of blood donation, the donation process, and how to maintain good health before and after donation.

Thank you for visiting our website and joining us in our mission to save lives through blood donation.




Why donate blood?

Blood donation is an essential part of human life, as it can save the life of many persons and biodiversity. Donating blood helps people with their health problems by replenishing essential fluids in the body, which can prevent deadly diseases. Now a days blood banks stores this blood in bottles which are supplied in the time of need. we must all come to donate the blood.

Our Vision

To create a platform that brings together donors, recipients, and healthcare providers in a seamless and efficient manner. We aim to create a user-friendly interface that not only encourages individuals to donate blood but also educates them on the importance and benefits of blood donation Ultimately, we create a community of dedicated donors who are committed to saving lives and improving health outcomes for people in need.

Our Goal

With the right donor data management and blood stock management, our goal is to work closely with blood banks to maintain their information and also recruit, engage and retain donors as per the demand. Folks in search of blood can get access to blood availability info.Ultimately, the goal is to save lives by encouraging more people to donate blood and ensuring that there is an adequate supply of blood for those in need.

Why US?

Existing blood management system in Nepal is manual, cumbersome and inefficient. Most blood banks record the information on blood collection/supply manually in registers.

Maintaining blood stock inventory is tedious with laborious back-office paperwork and managing information on availability and shortage of blood is a tall task.

A social initiative for a smart, transparent and holistic blood management service from collection to supply.

When it comes to blood, right information at the right time can be the answer to a life and death situation.
